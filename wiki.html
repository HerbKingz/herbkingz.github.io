Herbal Knowledgebase

Learn about the science, history, and uses of medical cannabis and supporting herbs.

CBD (Cannabidiol)

Non-psychoactive compound used for anxiety, pain relief, and inflammation. Found in oils and tinctures.

THC (Tetrahydrocannabinol)

Psychoactive component of cannabis. Used medicinally for pain, appetite stimulation, and nausea.

Indica vs Sativa

Indica strains tend to relax the body; Sativa strains are more energising. Hybrids offer a mix of both.

UK Legal Status

Medical cannabis is legal in the UK with a prescription. Recreational use remains prohibited.
